TOKENOMICS & ROYALTIES

    Our tokenomics model and the NFT price/reward relation can be seen in the plot below but lets also put it into words:

  1. The earlier you get in, actually the better: We would like to give back to the early adapters who chose to trust our project. The first 5000 will start on a higher price per NFT, but they will have a set reward which will be unlocked as more APAs are sold.
  2. Reward decreases with sales: Initially, there is a high reward that ultimately makes earlier purchases cheaper. As more APAs are sold, the reward decreases faster than the NFT price which effectively makes the actual paid price higher. To be more technical, reward curve drops faster than the NFT price curve. People who buy early get a higher discount and after the first 5000, the discount goes to zero.
  3. Reward distribution: There is a linear correlation between the number of NFT's sold and the percentage of rewards received. For example, when 50% is sold, you will be eligible to claim 50% of your rewards.
  4. 3% Royalties for original minters: The royalties will be following the EIP2981 standard. Since it's not an enforceable standard, any marketplace compliant with it will be able to "respect" it. Unfortunately, we have no control over what other marketplaces do and can only guarantee to uphold the standard in our own marketplace.
  5. Royalties in our own marketplace: We offer 3% for original minters (EIP2981), 2% for the community and 1% for the market.
